WHAT IS FLITEBOARD?

Imagine a water craft that flies smoothly above the water at exhilarating speeds.
It doesn’t rely upon wind or waves, and gives you the freedom to travel for up to 30km per charge.Imagine a water craft so responsive that even the most subtle movements bank turns or change your flying altitude; providing endless freedom and expression. Imagine a water craft that combines the efficiency of hydrofoil wings, with the latest in electric vehicle and battery technology — to create a fast, clean, quiet, powerful ride like nothing before it.

This is Fliteboard

INTRODUCING FLITEBOARD Fliteboard® is a high performance water craft that is packed with innovation and built from the highest quality materials. Three boards are available to suit different rider weights and needs.Fly quietly above the water for up to 1 hour 30 minutes.
Fliteboard isn’t reliant on wind or waves and it gives you the freedom to ride anywhere, any time, without wind or waves.It’s fast, quiet, and emission free!

Efoilaustralia is proud to be an authorised Fliteschool and Fliteboard partner.We offer a 1.5 hour lesson on the beautiful portion of Swan River, between the South Perth Foreshore and
Elizabeth Quay.This is a unique opportunity to try the most fun activity you can have on flat water in one of the best spot in Perth.
It’s honestly a thrilling exprience like no other!Our professional instructors goal is to get you foiling on your first Fliteschool experience with a special focus on safety. We have a range of options to build confidence and skills of beginners and high performance set up for expert riders.You won’t forget your first time foiling and no previous experience is required.
