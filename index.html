An Experience like no other

Efoil Australia is thrilled to be an official Flite partner, introducing the most exhilarating watersport activity in years to the South Perth foreshore. The fantastic aspect of Fliteboarding is that it doesn't require any prior experience, and our skilled instructors are dedicated to ensuring an incredible and safe efoil adventure for everyone.

Situated on the picturesque South Perth foreshore, our location offers incredible conditions paired with stunning views of the city skyline. We are excited and proud to be here, eagerly awaiting your visit for an unforgettable experience.
Operating seven days a week from 8am to 6pm, we look forward to welcoming you for an amazing adventure.
