An Experience like no other

Efoil Australia is very proud as an official Flite partner to bring you the most exciting watersport activity in years to hit the South Perth foreshore. The beauty about Fliteboarding is that no previous experience is required and our expert instructors will guarantee you an amazing and safe efoil adventure.

Our location on the South Perth foreshore stands for amazing conditions combined with the spectacular views of our beautiful city skyline. We are thrilled and delighted to be here and can’t wait for you to join us for an unforgettable experience.
We operate 7 days a week from 9am to 6pm.
